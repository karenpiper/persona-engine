# Persona Template

Every generated persona has two parts:

1. **Standard Persona** — the team-facing document used in briefs, design, product, and strategy discussions
2. **AI Simulation Extension** — the deeper behavioral layer that powers `/persona-chat` conversations

Both sections are generated together. The standard persona is what teams read and use. The simulation extension is what makes the persona come alive in conversation.
